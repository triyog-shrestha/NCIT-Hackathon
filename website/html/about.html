Our story

Built for the quiet struggles students don't always talk about

heAlth started with a simple observation: so many students and young people silently battle anxiety, depression, and burnout while juggling deadlines, exams, and everyday life — often without anyone noticing. We built heAlth to make professional support feel approachable, affordable, and judgement-free, so reaching out for help feels like a normal first step, not a last resort.

Whether it's the pressure of exams, the isolation of moving away from home, or the weight of depression that's hard to name, our licensed therapists meet people exactly where they are — online, on their schedule, at their pace.

How we help

Facing anxiety and depression, one step at a time

We focus on the realities of student life and everyday pressure — not generic advice.

🎓 For students

Exam stress, social pressure, and the fear of falling behind can build up quietly. Our therapists help students develop coping tools, manage panic before exams, and rebuild motivation — without judgement, and around their class schedule.

🧠 For anxiety

From racing thoughts to physical symptoms like a tight chest or sleepless nights, anxiety shows up differently for everyone. We match clients with therapists trained in CBT and grounding techniques that actually fit their life.

🌧️ For depression

Low energy, loss of interest, and a constant heaviness are signs worth taking seriously. Our therapists create a steady, non-judgemental space to talk honestly and rebuild momentum, one small step at a time.

🤝 For everyone else

Relationship struggles, burnout, grief, or simply feeling lost — you don't need a diagnosis to deserve support. heAlth welcomes anyone who wants someone to talk to.
